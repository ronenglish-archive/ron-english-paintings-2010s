Notes

The composition references Pablo Picasso’s Guernica.

The work incorporates pop-cultural, advertising, and civic iconography, including Fat Buddha imagery, Tony the Tiger, the Statue of Liberty, James Montgomery Flagg’s I Want You for U.S. Army, and Mickey Mouse.
