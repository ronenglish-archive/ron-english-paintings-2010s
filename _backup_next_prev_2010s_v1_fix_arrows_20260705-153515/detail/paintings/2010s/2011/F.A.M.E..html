Notes

Also documented as F.A.M.E. Chris Brown Cover.

Corey Helford Gallery documents this work under SCOPE Miami 2011. The gallery page lists the work as oil on canvas; Status Factory additionally records spray paint among the media.

This work references Chris Brown and was used for the cover of his album F.A.M.E., released in 2011.

This painting is shown in progress at 1:37 in SAFEWALLS’ Vimeo video SAFEWALLS | WHAT IS SAFEWALLS?.

It also appears in studio photographs published in Arrested Motion, “Studio Visits: Ron English – Skin Deep @ Lazarides Rathbone Place,” June 14, 2011.

The work appears in IBTimes UK’s coverage of Ron English and Chris Brown’s Dum English collaboration and in Getty Images’ editorial photographs of the Dum English exhibition opening at Opera Gallery, New York.
