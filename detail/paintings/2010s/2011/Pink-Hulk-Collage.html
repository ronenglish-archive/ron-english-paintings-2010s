Notes

This work references Ronald McDonald and the Incredible Hulk.

This work appears in exhibition photographs published in Alec Banks, “Ron English ‘English 101’ Exhibition @ POST NO BILLS Recap,” Hypebeast, October 7, 2011.

The work is also documented on Artnet’s artwork page for Pink Hulk.

Artsy, referencing Heritage Auctions, documents a closely related or possibly variant work under the title Pink Hulk, dated 2012, described as mixed media and collage on wove paper, measuring 11 × 9 inches and not signed. The catalogue metadata for this record has been retained as Pink Hulk Collage, 2011, oil, acrylic and collage on paper, 8.5 × 11 inches.
