It also appears in studio photographs published in Arrested Motion, “Studio Visits: Ron English – Skin Deep @ Lazarides Rathbone Place,” June 14, 2011.

Arrested Motion’s opening coverage of Skin Deep at Lazarides Gallery identifies this work as Comic Fred and lists the medium as oil, synthetic polymer paint and collage on canvas.
