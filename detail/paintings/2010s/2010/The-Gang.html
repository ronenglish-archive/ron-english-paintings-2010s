Notes

This work references Ronald McDonald.

This work appears on Kidrobot’s page for Popaganda in Japan, and in Kaiju Korner’s exhibition photographs of the same presentation.

A reference set assembled by Ron English is retained as supporting documentation for this work.

The work also appears in studio photographs published by Arrested Motion in “Studio Visits: Ron English @ Popaganda Headquarters,” May 26, 2009.

This composition was reproduced as one of the images included in the Ron English Postcard Set sold by Clutter; the product page describes the release as a package of eight postcards, each featuring a different piece of Ron English imagery.
