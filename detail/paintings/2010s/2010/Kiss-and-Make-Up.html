Notes

In Death and the Eternal Forever, this work was erroneously reported as 50 × 64 inches.

This work references Louis Jean François Lagrenée’s Rinaldo and Armida.

This painting also references members of the band KISS in their signature makeup.

This work appears at 2:01 in Highsnobiety TV’s YouTube video Ron English Talks Status Factory.
