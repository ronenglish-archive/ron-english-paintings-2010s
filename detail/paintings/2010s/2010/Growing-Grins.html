Notes

Ron English assembled and photographed a reference set as a model for this work.

This work is reproduced on the show card for Status Factory.

The image was later reproduced in several related objects and editions, including a blotter-paper edition, a ceramic art plate, and print-related documentation connected to Bense Art Space.

A work-in-progress image for this painting is retained in the catalogue documentation.
