Exhibitions

English Translation Series, Pop Life Global Q Plex, Shenzhen, China, June 21–July 14, 2019.

Literature

Ron English, English Translation Series (Dongguan City, Guangdong, PRC: PopLife GLOBAL, n.d.), p. 10.

Notes

The work is documented in the printed exhibition booklet English Translation Series, which also serves as evidence of its inclusion in the exhibition.

This work references Jean-Michel Basquiat’s Versus Medici and Everlast boxing gloves.

This composition was later reproduced in at least two separate print runs. A 1XRUN edition was released on June 11, 2021 as a 10-color screen print measuring 26 × 37 inches on hand-deckled 410gsm Somerset Satin Fine Art Paper, issued in an edition of 150, with a Printer Proof (PP) variant also listed. A separate earlier print run was documented on KKNews on July 2, 2019, listing Basquiat Boxer Everlast as a 24 × 36 inch print in an edition of 50.
