Notes

This work appears on Corey Helford Gallery’s artwork page for the exhibition Lucky 13 Anniversary Show, Pt. 1: Fine Art of Street & Graffiti.

The composition references the Peanuts character Snoopy, created by Charles M. Schulz, and DC Comics’ Batman.
