Notes

Dimensions are approximate and are based on visual comparison and available documentation.

This work references Leonardo da Vinci’s The Last Supper (c. 1495–1498).

The composition incorporates breakfast-cereal mascot imagery, including Sugar Bear, Snap, Crackle and Pop, Cap’n Crunch, the Trix Rabbit, Quisp, Toucan Sam, Tony the Tiger, Lucky the Leprechaun, Count Chocula, and Franken Berry.
