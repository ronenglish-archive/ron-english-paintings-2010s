Exhibitions

Madness, part of the Thailand Toy Expo, Sky Lobby of CentralWorld, Bangkok, Thailand, April 28–May 3, 2015.

Notes

This composition was adapted by the artist as a limited-edition lithograph issued in 2015. The print measured 87 × 60.50 cm (34.25 × 23.81 inches), was produced on White BFK Rives 270 g paper as a 6-color lithograph printed with Marinoni lithographic machines and hand cut, and was released in an edition of 99. The print was signed and numbered by the artist.
